Mütevazı Başlangıçlar

1952 yılında, Gaziantep'in tarihi şehrinde küçük bir aile mutfağında dedemiz Mehmet, bir mutfak mirası haline gelecek olan şeyi başlattı. Sadece nesillerden nesillere aktarılan geleneksel tarifler ve orijinalliğe olan sarsılmaz bağlılığıyla, yerel aileler ve komşular için kebap hazırlamaya başladı.

Anadolu mutfağının zengin lezzetlerini paylaşma konusundaki bu mütevazı çaba, kısa sürede çok daha büyük bir şeye dönüştü. Dar taş kaldırımlı sokaklar boyunca Mehmet'in kebaplarının olağanüstü tadı hakkında haberler yayıldı; her biri özenle seçilmiş baharatlarla hazırlanmış ve közde pişirilmişti.

A Philosophy Rooted in Tradition

Our culinary philosophy remains unchanged: respect for the ingredient, mastery of technique, and an unwavering commitment to authenticity. Every spice is sourced from the same regions our grandfather visited, every recipe follows his handwritten notes, and every dish is prepared with the same passion that ignited our family's journey.

Food is not just sustenance—it is memory, culture, and love served on a plate. When you taste our kebabs, you taste three generations of dedication.

Today, MZD Kebap ve Dürüm Evi stands as a bridge between the time-honored traditions of Turkish cuisine and the sophisticated palates of modern diners. We honor our heritage while embracing innovation, creating an experience that is both authentically Turkish and uniquely contemporary.

Our Journey

Seven decades of culinary excellence and family tradition

1952 - The Beginning

Mehmet opens his first small kitchen in Gaziantep, serving traditional kebabs to local families with recipes passed down through generations.

1978 - Growing Recognition

The family opens their first formal restaurant, gaining recognition for authentic Anatolian cuisine and becoming a destination for food lovers across the region.

1995 - Second Generation

Mehmet's son Ahmet takes over the business, expanding the menu while maintaining traditional cooking methods and introducing modern dining experiences.

2018 - Urban Expansion

The family opens MZD Kebap ve Dürüm Evi in the heart of the city, bringing their heritage recipes to urban food enthusiasts while maintaining authenticity.

2024 - Modern Legacy

The third generation continues the tradition, blending time-honored recipes with contemporary presentation, ensuring our legacy lives on for future generations.
